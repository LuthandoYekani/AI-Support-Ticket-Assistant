# Sample AI Technical Assessment

## Issue Summary

Customer reports a complete loss of internet connectivity on a residential fibre connection.

---

## Priority

**High**

Reason:

- Complete outage
- Business impact possible
- Troubleshooting already performed

---

## Likely Causes

1. Physical fibre break
2. ONT failure
3. Authentication issue
4. Account provisioning problem

---

## Recommended Troubleshooting

- Verify ONT LED status.
- Check optical power levels.
- Verify PPPoE authentication.
- Inspect fibre patch lead.
- Check customer account status.

---

## Escalation

Escalate to Fibre Network Operations if physical layer failure is confirmed.
